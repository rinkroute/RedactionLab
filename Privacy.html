RedactionLab Privacy Policy

Effective date: Jan 3rd 2026

Summary

RedactionLab is designed to process PDFs locally on your Mac. We do not sell personal information and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store your document contents.

RedactionLab does not require you to create an account.

How Your Data Is Used

  • PDF processing, redaction preview, and export occur on-device.
  • If OCR is enabled, text recognition is performed on your Mac to assist in locating content in scanned PDFs.

Files and Permissions

RedactionLab accesses files you explicitly choose to open and the location you choose to save exported PDFs.

Third-Party Services

RedactionLab does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising services.

Purchases (if offered) are handled by Apple via the App Store.
