Frequently Asked Questions

Does RedactionLab upload my documents?

No. RedactionLab processes PDFs locally on your Mac.

Why does OCR say it’s not perfect?

OCR accuracy depends on scan quality (resolution, contrast, skew, and compression). Always review results before exporting.

Best practices for scanned PDFs

  • Prefer scans around 300 DPI or higher
  • High contrast (dark text on light background)
  • Avoid motion blur and extreme skew

How do I make sure redactions are applied?

Use Redact All to preview black boxes, then Save Redacted… to export a flattened redacted PDF.
